Meet The team

Dr Dirk Schuth

Specialist Orthodontist

BDS FDSRCS FDSRCPS MOrth(Edin) MOrth(Eng)
MDentSci PgDipClinEd

Dirk Schuth is the team leader and owner of Trinity House Orthodontics. He is a specialist orthodontist and registered with the General Dental Council as a specialist in orthodontics. He is dedicated to the NHS aspect of his practice, to those who clinically require braces for health reasons, but equally is interested in the adult and aesthetic orthodontic side of the practice. He has vast experience with Invisalign and he and his team have treated in excess of 30,000 cases with fixed appliances over the last 15 years. He is versed with Incognito and HARMONY lingual appliances and is at the forefront of digital orthodontics using CBCT scanning, intraoral scanning and is the ONLY Suresmile provider in the North of England.
